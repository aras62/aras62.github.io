Datasets

    Pedestrian Intention Estimation (PIE)
    A dataset of 1842 pedestrians in more than 6 hours of on-board video recordingfor the purpose of analyzing pedestrians' intentions and behaviors at the point of crossing.
    P3 and O3
    A set of patters used in psychophysical research and a set of realistic odd-one-out stimuli gathered "in the wild" to evaluate the ability of saliency algorithms to find targets distinct from distractors
    Joint Attention in Autonomous Driving (JAAD)
    A dataset of 340+ clips of traffic scenes for the purpose of analyzing pedestrians' behaviors at the point of crossing.
    3D Gazebo Models (3DGEMS)
    A dataset of 270+ 3D models and world files designed for Gazebo simulation software
